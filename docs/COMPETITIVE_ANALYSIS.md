# Competitive Analysis: Forex Risk Calculator Platform

## Executive Summary

**Market Position:** We are **not competing** with trading platforms (MetaTrader, TradingView, cTrader) on execution. We are **complementary** - focusing on the **educational and compliance layer** that these platforms lack.

**Competitive Moat:**
1. **White-label focus:** Enterprise B2B vs. B2C freemium
2. **Compliance-first:** Audit trails and regulatory reporting built-in
3. **Educational integration:** Risk calculators paired with learning modules
4. **Broker partnership model:** Embedded in broker ecosystems (not standalone)

**Key Insight:** No direct competitor offers white-label risk education + compliance platform for brokers. We have a **12-18 month first-mover advantage** in this niche.
